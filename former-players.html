Hall of Fame

Special recognition for extraordinary contributions to our village cricket

1992: Inauguration of the Village Cricket Ground

Led by Mohan Singh and community volunteers, our modern cricket ground was established.

1998: First Regional Championship

Our team won their first regional championship under Captain Mohan Singh's leadership.

2005: Youth Development Program

Raman Patel's youth cricket program produced five players who later represented district teams.

2012: Women's Cricket Initiative

Kamala Devi started the village's first women's cricket team, breaking barriers in rural sports.
